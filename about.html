Gounibe will launch in Fall 2023. The idea behind Gounibe is to create a place where creativity, sustainability, and community converge. We are an independent upscaling brand sourced in the vibrant neighborhood of Islington. Our mission is to breathe new life into pre-loved clothes sourced from charity shops, transforming them into unique designs that resonate with your personal style.

At our core, we believe in the power of repurposing existing resources rather than contributing to the demand for new materials. By harnessing the potential of forgotten pieces, we not only reduce waste but also support various charities and communities that benefit from the sales of our products.

In our commitment to ethicality, we purposefully avoid brands known for their fast fashion practices, such as Primark, Shein, ASOS, Zara, and H&M. By distancing ourselves from their production processes, even within the realm of second-hand items, we uphold our values of sustainability and integrity. For this reason, our curated selection of second-hand clothing exhibits exceptional quality and craftsmanship, surpassing what is usually associated with the term "second-hand."

Every design in our collection is meticulously crafted by hand, ensuring attention to detail and an infusion of passion into each item. Our dedicated team invests considerable time and thought into creating pieces that transcend trends, embodying timeless style and unique character. To further exemplify our commitment to quality, the sewing of our brand's labels is also lovingly done by hand, adding an extra touch of authenticity and care to every item.

We believe in the spirit of collaboration and warmly welcome cloth donations from individuals who share our vision of sustainability and giving back. Moreover, we invite you to express your creativity by sharing specific design requests, enabling us to bring your fashion aspirations to life in a meaningful and eco-conscious way.

As we embark on this exciting journey, our inaugural collection will showcase ten wearable items, each one-of-a-kind and brimming with personality. To be among the first to experience our creations, we encourage you to join our waitlist. By signing up, you will have the chance to be randomly chosen and granted exclusive access to our remarkable designs.

Together, we can redefine the world of fashion by championing sustainability, empowering local charities, and celebrating the art of upcycling.
